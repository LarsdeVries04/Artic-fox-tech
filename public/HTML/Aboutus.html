Our story

Arctic Fox was born from a passion for innovation and a desire to revolutionize the workspace environment. Founded by tech enthusiasts and furniture craftsmen, we envisioned a company that seamlessly integrates technology and furniture to create innovative solutions.

Our Mission

At Arctic Fox, we are dedicated to offering high-quality computer parts, custom-designed desks, and cutting-edge desk-integrated computers. Our mission is to empower individuals and businesses with tailor-made solutions that elevate their computing experience. Through craftsmanship, innovation, and personalized service, we strive to exceed expectations and inspire creativity.

Our vision

Our vision is to revolutionize the workspace environment by seamlessly integrating technology and furniture, providing innovative solutions that enhance productivity and comfort. We aim to lead the way in creating workspaces that inspire creativity and productivity.

Arctic Fox Company

Arctic Fox is a Netherlands-based company located in Rijnsburg, with a team of 30 dedicated employees. We are committed to sustainable production practices, ensuring that our operations have minimal impact on the environment while delivering high-quality products.
